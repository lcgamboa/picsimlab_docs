6.4 qemu-ESP32

“Qemu ESP32: Qemu Emulator for TTGO TDisplay esp32 board. ”

For integrated use with the Arduino IDE or IDF esptool.py , simply configure the serial port as explained in the Chapter Serial Communication to flash PICSimLab ESP32-DevKitC as a real ESP32 board.

Atention! Qemu ESP32 don´t support the QIO and QOUT flash modes, use only DIO or DOUT flash modes.
